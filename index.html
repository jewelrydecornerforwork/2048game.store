How to Play 2048

1

Move Tiles

Use arrow keys (desktop) or swipe (mobile) to move all tiles in one direction.

2

Combine Numbers

When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with double the value.

3

Reach 2048

Keep combining tiles to create larger numbers. The goal is to create a tile with the number 2048.

4

Keep Going

After reaching 2048, you can continue playing to achieve even higher scores and bigger tiles!

Pro Tips for Success

• Choose a corner: Keep your highest tile in a corner and build around it.

• Don't chase small tiles: Focus on building larger tiles systematically.

• Think ahead: Consider the consequences of each move before making it.

• Stay calm: Don't panic when the board fills up - there's usually a way out!
